About Me

Hello! My name is Emma Caliendo and I am the creator of this fine website. I created this website for my introduction to computer science class for my final project. I chose to make this website about Labrador Retreivers because I have one myself named Roxie. She is a three year old Yellow English Labrador Retreiver.

About Roxie

Roxie is a three year old Yellow Labrador Retriever. She has hazel eyes and a small brown nose. Roxie loves to lay in the sun during the day and curl up into a ball at night. Her favorite toy is her Rat King plush with a long tail that squeaks. Roxie is the best dog ever but somethimes she can be annoying like when she jumps on people.
